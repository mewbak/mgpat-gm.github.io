Some Notes on Shopping for Items: June 28, 2020

I have calculated some commonly sought after items that can be shopped in town. I do not know the probabilities of shopping items in town. Therefore, some of these might be easier to find in the dungeon. Others can only be found in shops. Some of these represent the assumed ideal level and others the lowest possible level. With Gris I am aiming for the maximum amount of slot availability at the lowest character level, and the minimum clvl at Wirt or Adria. These may or may not be the most probable character level to find the items.

Most other sought after items not on this list are found in d15. This list is not exhaustive(yet!).
